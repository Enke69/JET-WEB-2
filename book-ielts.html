Book Your IELTS Test

Register for computer-delivered IELTS at Mongolia's official British Council test centre.

How to Book

Booking Process

1

Register Online

Visit ieltsregistration.britishcouncil.org and create your account. Select your preferred test date and type (Academic or General Training).

2

Make Payment

Transfer 899,000 MNT to Jettrack/ЖЭТТРАКК ХХК — TDB Account: 499 232 558 or Khaan Bank: 503 127 2734

3

Send Confirmation

Email your payment proof and registration reference number to ielts@jet-english.mn within 24 hours.

4

Receive Confirmation

You will receive a confirmation email with your test date, time, and venue details.

Important

Before You Book

Required Documents

Valid passport (original required on test day), recent passport-sized photo, registration confirmation email

Test Formats

Computer-delivered IELTS: Listening, Reading, and Writing are completed on a computer. Speaking is face-to-face with an examiner.

Results

Results are available 3-5 days after your test for computer-delivered IELTS. You can view them online through your British Council account.

Cancellation Policy

You can cancel or transfer your test up to the deadline shown in your confirmation email. Cancellation fee: 230,000 MNT.
