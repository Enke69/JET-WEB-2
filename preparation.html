Study Tips

Preparation Tips

🎧

Listening Tips

Practice with English podcasts and news daily. Focus on understanding accents from the UK, Australia, and North America.

📖

Reading Tips

Read academic journals and newspapers regularly. Practice skimming and scanning techniques to improve speed.

✍️

Writing Tips

Practice writing Task 1 and Task 2 essays regularly. Focus on structure, coherence, and vocabulary range.

🗣️

Speaking Tips

Record yourself speaking and review. Practice discussing a wide range of topics fluently and naturally.

📚

Vocabulary

Build academic vocabulary systematically. Learn words in context rather than memorising lists.

⏱️

Time Management

Practice under timed conditions. Learn to allocate your time effectively across all sections.
